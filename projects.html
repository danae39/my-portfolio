NutriVerify - Smart Nutritional Fact Checking

🥗 NutriVerify

Smart Nutritional Fact Checking Platform

Presented by: Daniela Cherono

🚨 Problem Statement

Social media spreads conflicting and unscientific claims concerning nutrition, leading to:

Elimination of Food Groups

Trend diets often recommend cutting out entire food groups (carbs, fats, dairy) without scientific justification, leading to nutritional deficiencies.

Unbalanced Macronutrients

Extreme ratios of proteins, fats, and carbohydrates that don't align with physiological needs or established dietary guidelines.

Inadequate Micronutrients

Focus on macronutrients often ignores essential vitamins and minerals critical for metabolic health and disease prevention.

🎯 The Critical Gap

No system exists to evaluate nutrition claims based on actual nutrient composition and dietary reference intakes

The Vicious Cycle

Trend-Based Choices → Unverified Claims → Nutrient Imbalances

✨ Proposed Solution: NutriVerify

A comprehensive nutritional fact-checking platform that bridges the gap between nutrition science and consumer choices.

1

Submit Claim

User submits a diet or nutritional claim

2

Identification

System identifies foods and nutrients involved

3

Estimation

Nutrient intake is calculated and estimated

4

Comparison

Intake compared to recommended allowances

5

Results

User receives adequacy score and corrections

🛡️ Key Features

  • ✅ Verification using scientific databases (USDA, FoodData Central)
  • 🚩 Automatic flagging of false or misleading claims
  • 📊 Evidence-based nutrition guidance and recommendations
  • 📱 User-friendly interface with mobile accessibility
  • 🔒 Integration with dietary reference intakes (DRI) and AMDR
